Before You Book

To keep everyone safe and happy, please note:

Weight Limit

Dogs must be under 60 lbs. This helps me safely handle your pup in all conditions, especially on winter sidewalks.

Aggression History

If your dog has any history of aggression toward people or other dogs, they must be muzzled before I arrive. I reserve the right to decline walks if I feel unsafe.

Your Leash

Please have your own standard leash ready (4-6 feet, no retractable leashes). Make sure it's in good condition.

Health

Dogs must be current on rabies vaccination. If your dog is showing signs of illness (coughing, vomiting, etc.), please keep them home.
